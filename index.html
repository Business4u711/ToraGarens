Our Story

A Pioneer of Garden
Estate Dining

Tora Gardens was born from a simple yet revolutionary vision: to bring the luxury of open-air, garden-estate dining to the heart of Thika. As one of the first restaurants of its kind within a residential estate, we reimagined what dining out could truly feel like.

Our serene environment — infused with cool, crisp air and lush greenery — brings tranquility while you dine. Whether it's an outing with close friends, a family celebration, or a quiet afternoon escape, Tora Gardens is where memories are made.
